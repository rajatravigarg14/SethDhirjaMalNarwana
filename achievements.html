Achievements

Naranjan Lal Gupta
First BA & BT (B.Ed) Punjab State
Naranjan Lal S/O Nand Lal S/O Harsharan Das S/O Kanhaiya Lal S/O Devi Ditta Mal S/O Sada Sukh Mal S/O Dhirja Mal
Sh. Naranjan Lal Gupta was born on 2.11.1908. He was first BA & BT (B.Ed) in Punjab State. He did his BA from Punjab University Lahore in 1928-29 and BT from Banaras in 1929-30. He was first posted as Headmaster in High School Patiala in 1930. He received multiple letters from Congress Leaders in 1945 & 1946.
