Dear Family Members,
We all feel proud of this thing that we are the decendents of Seth Dhirja Mal Garg of Narwana. The purpose of this website is to bring all our family members closer as well as to familiarise all our younger ones to our ancesters and our glorious past.

Happy Birthday Seth Dhirja Mal
What a magnificient personality Seth Dhirja Mal was! He was a posthomous child. His father Lala Ghasi Ram s/o Lala Jai Gopal left for his heavenly abode leaving his bereaved widow who wanted to suicide. An unknown Sage consoled her and told that her son would not only be successful in his life but would be remembered for ages by his decendents and the people of Narwana. And it proved true.

Chirag Printing Press, Narwana


New India Senior Secondary School, Panchkula

Seth Dhirja Mal used all his resources for the benefit of the people of the surrounding areas. He was known for his sincerity and generosity. He helped Maharaja Patiala with money and acquired the title of Seth. Maharaja Patiala gave big portions of land to him. More than 250 years have passed and now his 12th generation has come but till today he is remembered by the people of Narwana as well as by his decendents.
Our ancesters have done countless acts of charity for the people of Narwana. Wells were dug to solve the problem of drinking water for the people as well as arrangements were made for the food and water for animals, Ghats were made for bathing, a Dharamshala was built for the tired travellers and a temple was also built for one and all. To solve the problem of drinking water of nearby areas, 17 acers of land was given to the Government of Haryana for a Jalghar.
Our ancesters donated land as well as money for educational institutions. Seth Patram laid the foundation of Arya Samaj Narwana and Arya Kanya Pathshala. These schools have installed big portrait of Seth Patram in their offices. A vast locality of Narwana is known as Patram Nagar in honour of Seth Patram. Today also our family members are engaged in so many charity works.
