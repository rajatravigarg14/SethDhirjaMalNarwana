Birthday Celebrations @ 2019

New Borns
One most awaited occasion, the Birthday of our great ancestor Seth Dhirja Mal, was celebrated with fun and enjoyment in the cordial family environment at our family Dharashala on 5th Oct. 2019. More than 250 members of the family assembled and enjoyed the celebration which ended with a delicious lunch.
Havan & Shradhanjali
As usual the event started with a havan for the peace and prosperity of the Dhirja Family. After havan all the family members gave shradhanjali to Seth Dhirja Mal and got the blessings.
Antakshari and Songs
Antakshari, a game of filmi songs was enjoyed by one and all. Jeevan Prakash, Gautam Garg and kids of the family sang sweet songs.Funny tonge-twisters entertained all.
Dance & Funny Questions
Beautiful dance performances were given by the grand daughters of DR. Vinod Gupta and Mahavir Garg. One great attraction was funny questions with amazing videos. The celebration ended with Pritibhoj.
Tambola
The most awaited event was tambola which was hosted by Ravi Bhushan Garg & Rajat Ravi Garg.
News Published
Next day local Newspapers elaborately published about the get-together of Dhirja Khandan.
New Borns
The newly born family members were introduced by Ravi Bhushan Garg and Rajat Ravi Garg. The New Additions were cheered with blessings and clappings.
