Birthday Celebrations @ 2018

Havan & Shradhanjali
Hit with Stick Game
Musical Chair Race
Website Launch
Tambola, Dance & Funny Questions
Birthday of Seth Dhirja Mal was celebrated on 2nd October 2018 in Dhirja Dharamshala Narwana. All the family members enjoyed a lot. There were many events like fun acivity, website launch and games. Celebration ended with a delicious lunch. Togetherness and unity of family made this celebration a great succes.
Havan & Shradhanjali
Event started with a havan for the peace and prosperity of the Dhirja Family. After havan all the family members gave shradhanjali to Seth Dhirja Mal.
Hit with Stick Game
Hit with the stick game was enjoyed by one and all.
Musical Chair Race
Musical Chair race was organised for children and ladies of the family. Prizes were distributed to the winners.
Website Launch
Seth Dhirja Mal Website was launched by Rajat Ravi Garg S/O Ravi Bhushan Garg Advocate. The website was highly appreciated with clappings.
Tambola, Dance & Funny Questions
The last event was tambola which was hosted by Mr. Ravi Bhushan Garg & Mr. Ghanshyam Garg. The event ended with funny questions and a beautiful dance performance given by the grand daughter of Mohinder Pal Garg. The celebration ended with lunch.
