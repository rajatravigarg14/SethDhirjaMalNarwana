Birthday Celebrations @ 2018

Havan & Shradhanjali
Hit with Stick Game
Musical Chair Race
Website Launch & 1 Minute Ques
Tambola & Dance
Birthday of Seth Dhirja Mal was celebrated on 2nd October 2018 in Dhirja Dharamshala Narwana. All the family members enjoyed a lot. There were many events like fun acivity, website launch and games. Celebration ended with a delicious luch. Togetherness and unity of family made this celebration a great succes.
Havan & Shradhanjali
Event started with a havan for the peace and prosperity of the Dhirja Family. After havan all the family members gave shradhanjali to Seth Dhirja Mal.
Hit with Stick Game
This was a fun acitivity for all the children. Each member was blindfolded and they needed to hit a tokri with a stick. Member with maximum hit were given prizes.
Musical Chair Race
Musical Chair race was was organised for children and ladies of the family. Prizes were distributed to each winner.
Website Launch & 1 Minute Ques
Seth Dhirja Mal Website was launched by Rajat Ravi Garg followd by 1 Minute Questions. Family members need to answer some funny twisted common sende questions. Small prizes were distributed for the correct answers.
Tambola & Dance
The last event was tambola which was hosted by Mr. Ravi Bhushan Garg. The event ended with a beautiful dance performance given by family member. The celebration ended with lunch ceremony.
