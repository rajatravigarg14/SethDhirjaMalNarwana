Dear Family Members,
Our first family reunion took place on 2nd October, 1999 at Dhirja Dharamshala,Narwana. More than 750 members of our family assembelled to celeberate one day in honour of our great ancester Seth Dhirja Mal. From that year, inspite of obstacles, 2nd October is celeberated every year as the Birthday of Seth Dhirja Mal. Hundreds of our family members collect to get the blessings of our great-great-grand father. After doing Hawan and paying homage to Seth Dhirja Mal, cultural programme and family interaction takes place. Family members from 6th generation to 11th generation assemble and share love and experiences. After this, all cordially enjoy Pritibhoj.
Since we have started celeberating this day, the reputation of our family has increased in Narwana. Our relations have strenthened. We have come in contact with some such members of our family who were not in contact. All of our family members should come and attend this family reunion programme.
